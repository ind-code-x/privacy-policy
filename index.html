Privacy Policy for SareeLuv

At SareeLuv, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device information (such as model and OS)
  • Camera or Gallery image uploads (for fabric/saree detection)
  • Location data (only when searching nearby stores)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to power fabric recognition, product suggestions, and store finder functionality. We do not share or sell your data.

3. Permissions

We request camera, photo library, and location access only for functionality, not for tracking.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google and Firebase APIs, which may collect anonymized data as per their terms.

5. Data Retention

We do not store personal data unless explicitly submitted by the user.

6. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at support@indcodex.com
